Ghost Notes

Save articles from anywhere.

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any page to save it to your Ghost Notes Haul instantly.

Save to Ghost Notes

How to install

  1. 1 Desktop: Drag the orange button above to your bookmarks bar.
    Mobile: Tap and hold the button, then choose "Add Bookmark."
  2. 2 Navigate to any article you want to save.
  3. 3 Click your "Save to Ghost Notes" bookmark. Done!

💡 Mobile Safari: enable the bookmarks bar in Settings → Safari → Show Favorites Bar
